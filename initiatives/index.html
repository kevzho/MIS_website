Bringing people from underrepresented backgrounds is what makes our community unique.

We make a community to encourage people with underrepresented backgrounds in STEM to gain a network and opportunities in fields they have not been exposed to.

Bringing people all over the world and inspiring students is the main goal of this organization, and it is this diverse community that brings together our organization.

What this creates

  • Students learning about fields through science resources and podcasts.
  • Connections with bloggers, professionals, speakers, and ambassadors.
  • Projects, presentations, and real-world experience for students of all ages.
  • A network where students can ask questions and become more connected than ever before.
