Inclusivity

Turning inspiration into concrete opportunities.

Creating our own Slack channel to our upcoming ambassador program, we are advancing the inspiration of our students into something concrete like creating projects, presentations, and finding real-world experience with people to interact with and make students of all ages learn and find paths to success.

We make a community to encourage people with underrepresented backgrounds in STEM to gain a network and opportunities in fields they have not been exposed to.

Bringing people all over the world and inspiring students is the main goal of this organization, and it is this diverse community that brings together our organization.

We create opportunities for everyone to be able to join in and learn about different fields through our science and podcasts that they might not have access to online. With our network, students can ask questions and become more connected than ever before with the bloggers and professionals.

  • Slack channels and upcoming ambassador programs.
  • Projects, presentations, and real-world experience.
  • Access to science content, podcasts, bloggers, and professionals.
  • A global network where students can ask questions and find paths to success.
