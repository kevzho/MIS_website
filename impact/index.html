Worlds FRC Reach

Meeting global STEM communities at FIRST Robotics Worlds.

Our presence at Worlds FRC helped Minorities in STEM connect with students, robotics teams, and community leaders from across the global STEM ecosystem.

  • Shared the MIS mission with students already building in competitive robotics.
  • Strengthened visibility for underrepresented student leadership in STEM spaces.
  • Built momentum for chapters, partnerships, and future community programs.
