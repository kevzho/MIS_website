Mission + Goal

An initiative to bridge the gap in STEM.

Founded in 2024 at Woodward Academy, Minorities in STEM was created to bridge the gap between minorities and STEM by building access, community, and opportunity for underrepresented students.

  • Facilitate conversations within the STEM community.
  • Bring in guest speakers and input from people in countless fields and backgrounds.
  • Build a network of people all over the world to encourage future generations.
